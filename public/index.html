The Practice

The Science of Absolute Focus

Tratak is a traditional meditation practice from the yogic tradition involving sustained, gentle focus of the gaze on a single point. Practised for generations, Tratak is known to encourage mindfulness, concentration, and self-awareness.

During this registered Guinness World Records™ attempt, participants will follow a 2-minute guided Tratak session. All you need is a smartphone, an Instagram account, and a willingness to be still.

Your Guide

Guided by Himalayan Siddhaa Akshar

Himalayan Siddhaa Akshar (Grand Master Akshar) is an internationally recognised yoga teacher, spiritual leader, author, and Guinness World Records™ title holder. Under his guidance, Akshar Yoga Kendraa has set 21 Guinness World Records™ across multiple International Yoga Day events.
